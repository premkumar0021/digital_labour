Digital Labour Card Platform

Fair Employment • Transparency • Social Security

Empowering Unorganized Workers Digitally

A technology-driven platform that provides digital labour cards, skill-based job matching, GPS attendance tracking, automated wage calculation, and PF support to ensure fair and transparent employment.

Key Features

Digital Labour Card

Verified digital identity for workers with skills and experience.

Skill-Based Hiring

Employers can hire workers based on expertise and availability.

GPS Attendance

Accurate check-in and check-out with location verification.

Automated Wages

Automatic working hours and overtime calculation.

PF & Social Security

Provident fund and financial security support for workers.

Reduced Middlemen

Direct connection between workers and employers.
